About Us

Jaypee Institute of Information Technology is organizing a one day Electronics festival "Vidyut 2018" on  November 24, 2018. "Vidyut - Electronics with Youth", first ever of its kind aiming to encourage students participating in the Electronics fest and widen their horizon.

The event will provide ample opportunities to students to showcase their technical skills in the institute. There are various events like Project Exhibition, TechBuzz, Voltz and Technical Talks. All the events will help students in different fields to learn different skills.
